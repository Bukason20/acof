About Us

Powering Innovation & Engineering Solutions Since Inception

Who We Are

Acof is a fully registered Nigerian engineering and technical services company established to deliver high-quality, innovative, and cost-effective solutions across Electrical Engineering, Instrumentation & Control, Mechanical Engineering, Welding and Fabrication, Air Conditioning Systems, and General Consultancy Services.
We are strategically headquartered in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria, with operational capacity that extends to other regions of the country.

Our Story

Founded by a team of skilled engineers and professionals, Acof Technical Coy Limited was created to bridge the gap between local capability and international engineering standards.

Over the years, we have evolved from a small technical support company into a multi-disciplinary engineering solutions provider, serving clients in oil and gas, power, manufacturing, and public infrastructure.

Our Corporate Identity

We pride ourselves on being a client-focused organization driven by technology, professionalism, and strong ethical standards.

Our operations are guided by ISO-recognized standards ensuring every project is executed safely and efficiently.

Our Core Competencies

  • Electrical Engineering – Design, installation, and maintenance.
  • Instrumentation & Control – Process automation, PLC, and SCADA.
  • Mechanical Engineering – Equipment installation and maintenance.
  • Welding & Fabrication – Structural works, tanks, and pipelines.
  • HVAC Systems – Design and installation for residential and industrial clients.
  • General Consultancy – Technical advisory and safety audits.
